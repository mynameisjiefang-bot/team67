About FinLit Buddy

Helping young adults make clearer financial decisions.

FinLit Buddy is a school prototype built to support young adults entering the workforce. It focuses on budgeting, CPF awareness, financial product evaluation, and early-career money planning.

Our purpose

Why we built FinLit Buddy

Finlit Buddy is brought to you by Team 67 — Darius, Jaseem, Janelle, Randall, and Ricardo. We noticed something troubling among our peers: too many young Singaporeans are living paycheck to paycheck, with little to no savings set aside. That observation became the spark for Finlit Buddy — our answer to making financial literacy feel less like a chore and more like a habit worth building early. Our goal is simple: help youths make sense of their money, one conversation at a time — so payday stress becomes a little less of a monthly ritual.

Clear guidance

We simplify financial topics so users can understand key ideas before making decisions.

Trusted sources

The assistant is designed to use reliable sources such as MAS, CPF Board, and MoneySense instead of unverified online advice.

Career-stage focus

The platform is built around the needs of young adults entering their first full-time or part-time jobs.
