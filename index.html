What it does

A financial buddy, not random online advice.

The goal is to help users make career-stage-appropriate financial decisions without relying on guesswork, social media advice, or unverified opinions.

💰

Budgeting guidance

Helps users estimate how much to allocate to needs, wants, savings, and emergency funds.

🏦

CPF awareness

Explains CPF-related concepts in beginner-friendly language and encourages source checking.

🛡️

Safer financial decisions

Encourages users to check official sources before acting on financial claims, products, or advice.

📊

Product comparison

Encourages users to compare fees, risks, lock-in periods, and suitability before choosing financial products.
